## 2. Data Collected by Tasker Itself

Tasker is designed to collect as little data as possible. Specifically:

### 2.1 Nickname
The App may ask you for a nickname. This is used solely so the App can greet you and personalize its tone. This nickname:
- Is stored **locally on your device only**
- Is **never transmitted** to us or any third-party server
- Is never used for identification, analytics, or advertising purposes

### 2.2 GitHub Account Connection (Optional)
Tasker offers an optional feature to connect your GitHub account. This is used exclusively to increase your GitHub API rate limit when the App or its plugins fetch data from GitHub (for example, when checking for plugin updates).

- This connection is **entirely optional** and the App is fully functional without it
- Authentication is handled via GitHub's own OAuth device flow; we do not see or store your GitHub password
- Any access token issued is stored **locally on your device** and is used only to authenticate your own requests directly to GitHub's API
- We do not operate a backend server that receives, logs, or processes this token

### 2.3 No Server-Side Collection
Tasker's core functionality does not include a backend server. We do not collect, receive, or store any of the above information on our own infrastructure. All data described in this section stays on your device unless you choose to sync it via a system-level service (see Section 3).

## 3. Third-Party Services

- **GitHub API**: If you use the optional GitHub connection, your device communicates directly with GitHub. Your use of GitHub is governed by [GitHub's own Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).
- We do not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs in Tasker itself.

## 4. Plugins: Important Security and Privacy Notice

**This section applies to any plugin you install and is the most important part of this policy.**

Tasker is built around a plugin architecture that intentionally grants plugins broad access so that plugin developers are not restricted in what they can build. This means:

### 4.1 Tasker Has No Plugin Sandbox
Tasker does **not** sandbox, restrict, or monitor what a plugin can do once installed. A plugin may, without further notice to you:
- Read, modify, or delete data on your device, including data belonging to other plugins or the App itself
- Make network requests to any server, including sending your data to third parties
- Access system resources available to the App

### 4.2 Plugins Are Independent Third-Party Software
Each plugin is created and published by its own developer, who is solely responsible for:
- What data the plugin collects
- Where that data is sent, if anywhere
- Whether the plugin has its own privacy policy or terms

**We do not review, audit, or certify the security or data practices of third-party plugins before or after installation**.

### 4.3 No Liability for Plugin Behavior
Because Tasker is built by design to give plugins this level of access, **Rosepen Studios cannot be held responsible or liable for any data collection, data loss, security breach, or damage caused by a third-party plugin**, including but not limited to:
- Unauthorized data transmission
- Malicious code
- Data corruption or loss
- Vulnerabilities introduced into your device or accounts

### 4.4 Your Responsibility
Before installing any plugin, you should:
- Only install plugins from developers you trust
- Review the plugin's own privacy policy or documentation, if available
- Understand that you are granting that plugin broad access to your device by installing it

If you believe a plugin is behaving maliciously, please contact us at rosepen.sprt@gmail.com so we can consider removing it from any official listing, though removal does not undo effects already caused by the plugin.

## 5. Data Retention

Since Tasker does not transmit or store your data on our servers, we do not retain any of your data. The nickname and any GitHub token remain on your device until you delete them.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Because we do not collect or process your personal data on our servers, most GDPR data-subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability) are automatically satisfied — that data already lives only on your device, under your control.

If you have any questions or concerns about your data, you may still contact us at rosepen.sprt@gmail.com. If you believe your rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
